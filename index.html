About Us

ReTrends Boutique

Welcome to ReTrends Boutique — a quaint, modern clothing and accessories boutique tucked inside Gemini Complex, Polachery Road, Mambakkam, Chennai.

We offer a large and varied collection of trendy clothing and accessories at very reasonable prices. Whether you’re looking for everyday wear or something special, we have styles to suit every taste and budget.

  • Trendy, modern styles at affordable prices
  • Value for money — excellent quality products
  • Friendly staff, welcoming atmosphere
  • Located in Gemini Complex, near DABC Arboretum
